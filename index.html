02 · Firm

We invest and advise selectively, aligning capital and judgment to build permanent value

William Hewitt & Company is a private, long-term acquisition firm. Free from the constraints of fixed-term funds, we evaluate companies based on their compounding potential over decades rather than artificial exit timelines. Our mandate is straightforward: protect equity, optimize underlying unit economics, and respect the legacies of the businesses we join.

Our operational intervention is similarly restrained. We do not disrupt stable organizations for the sake of activity. Instead, we isolate the specific financial and commercial levers that build compounding enterprise value without introducing structural risk.

Where we focus
  • Private enterprises with stable market positions
  • Earnings durability and structural cash conversion
  • Operating models with high operational transferability
  • Low capital intensity with high structural margins
  • Pathways to organic and sustainable growth
03 · Approach
